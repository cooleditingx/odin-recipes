Tiramisu

Description

This is my version of Italian no-bake Tiramisu with espresso dipped Ülker tea biscuits and a homemade cream mix topped with some cocoa powder. This is the most amazing dessert with it's beautiful layers and sweet, fluffy taste.

Ingredients

  • 6oz of Ülker tea biscuits
  • 2 cups of Heavy cream
  • 14oz of Condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoon of Instant coffee
  • 1 cup of Warm water
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Take warm water, add coffe and mix it until it is dissolved. Set aside the coffee mixture.
  2. Put heavy cream in a bowl and whisk it until medium peaks form.
  3. Add condensed milk in heavy cream and fold it with a spatula.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of the coffee mixture in the cream and fold it.
  5. dip the biscuits in coffee mixture.
  6. Add a layer of biscuits on a rectangular glass casserole.
  7. After the first layer of biscuits,add 1/3 of the cream mixture and smooth the layer with an offset spatula.
  8. Add another layer of biscuits and cream.
  9. Then add another layer of biscuits and the remaining cream.
  10. Smooth the cream and dust it with some cocoa powder.
  11. Chill it overnight.
  12. Cut square pieces and Enjoy!
